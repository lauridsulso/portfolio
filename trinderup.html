Rasmus Trinderup

Rasmus Trinderup was a project made for a freelance guitar artist. The website was made to promote Rasmus as a guitar player, and to improve his number of bookings as a guitar teacher.
The website was mainly made using html/css, with a tiny bit of API(JS), to fetch postal codes, when the users make use of the contact form.
